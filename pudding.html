Healthy Caramel Pumpkin Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups plain, Unsweetened Coconut Yogurt
  • ¾ cup 100% Pure Pumpkin Puree
  • ⅓ cup Homemade Caramel Sauce (or Date Caramel Sauce)
  • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Xanthan Gum

Instructions

  1. In a blender, add the yogurt, pumpkin puree, caramel sauce, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, and salt. Blend until smooth
  2. While blending, add the xanthan gum. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour into serving jars, cover, and refrigerate overnight. Serve with coconut whipped cream and an extra drizzle of caramel sauce, or serve as is!
