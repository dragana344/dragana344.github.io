Healthy Peppermint Cheesecake

Ingredients

Chocolate Base:

  • 2 cups Walnuts
  • 1 cup Dates
  • ¼ cup Unsweetened Natural Cocoa Powder
  • ¼ tsp Salt

Cheesecake Filling:

  • 2 cups Walnuts
  • 3 oz Organic Raw Cacao Butter (melted)
  • two 13.5oz cans Let’s Do Organic Heavy Coconut Cream
  • 3 tbs Beet Juice (from canned beets)
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Paste
  • 2 tsp Liquid Stevia Extract
  • 2 tsp Peppermint Flavor
  • ⅛ tsp Salt

Instructions

For the Chocolate Base:

  1. In a food processor or high-speed blender (I used my Vitamix), add the walnuts and dates. Grind until it comes together and yields a moist crumble.
  2. Add the cocoa powder and salt and grind again. It’s ready when you can pinch it together and it stays together in a “dough.”
  3. Press the mixture into a 9” springform pan.

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  1. In a food processor or high-speed blender, add the walnuts and melted cacao butter. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the coconut cream, beet juice, vanilla paste, stevia extract, peppermint flavor, and salt. Blend until completely smooth.
  3. Pour over the crust. Tap the pan on the counter a few times to remove any air pockets. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Slice, serve, and enjoy!
