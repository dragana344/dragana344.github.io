Travel


Spain - Barcelona


Boasting a reputation as one of the most attractive cities in Europe, Barcelona celebrates its role as the capital of Catalonia. The city’s cosmopolitan and international vibe makes it a favorite city for many people around the world. The city is especially known for its architecture and art—travelers flock from around the world to see the iconic Sagrada Família church and other modernist landmarks designed by Gaudí.

Italy - Milan


Renowned for its mix of historical charm and modern style, Milan stands as Italy's fashion and design capital. The city's fashionable appeal attracts visitors from across the globe, eager to experience its lively cultural scene. Milan's architectural marvels, such as the majestic Duomo di Milano, captivate travelers with their timeless beauty, especially when illuminated against the night sky. The beautiful streets, lined with classic buildings and cozy cafes, have a unique charm that enchants every visitor. Celebrated for its art, fashion, and delicious food, Milan effortlessly blends the old with the new, making it a beloved destination for tourists and trendsetters alike.

Switzerland - beauty of the roads


Traveling through Switzerland is like driving through a postcard. The country's natural beauty is unmatched, and its roads offer a stunning showcase of the landscape. As I journeyed from Winterthur to St. Gallen, each bend in the road revealed breathtaking views. The well-maintained highways wind through lush valleys, beside crystal-clear lakes, and up into the majestic Swiss Alps. Also the traditional Swiss farms, visible from the road, add a rustic charm to the journey.
